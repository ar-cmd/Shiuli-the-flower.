For Shiuli

the word

I have been thinking about what passed between us, and I do not want it to sit there unspoken any longer.

I am sorry. Whatever I got wrong, however I made things harder instead of easier between us, that was not my intention, and it does not undo the fact that it happened.

I am not writing this to be forgiven on the spot, or to erase the discomfort with a nice page and some nice words. I am writing it because you deserved better than the misunderstanding, and I would rather say so plainly than let it linger unsaid.

the verse
Shiuli

Night flower, you open when no one is looking,
give your whole self to the dark, ask nothing back.
By morning you have already let go,
scattered, unbothered, honest.

I want to be that honest.
Not the flower that clings past its hour,
but the one that says what it means
and lets the saying be enough.

with plain apology, and no expectations
